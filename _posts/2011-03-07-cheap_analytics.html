Motivation

Google Analytics is an awesome product. Its only flaw, in my opinion, is that the aggregation of data is controlled by a single corporate entity.

What if there was a public benefit equivalent, which offered the must-have analytics features that 80% of sites need, but where the data was pooled, anonymized, aggregated, for the benefit of all?

Implementation notes

  • It need not be hosted from a single domain -- much of the hosting could be federated and aggregated (which would be good given the complex jurisdictional questions around data.
  • It need not be free for all uses.

Status

Just a blue sky dream.
