Motivation

Business Model Generation is a very compelling presentation of how to think and talk about business models that non-business types can understand. In particular, I found the canvas model highly generative, both solo and in conversations.

I got to wondering whether it would be possible to create a webapp to keep track of my business model canvases, and in particular whether I could use such an app to foster contribution by business thinkers in Mozilla efforts.

Status

Since I first thought about this, the folks behind the book have build an iPad app, which I'm sure is useful, and apparently they're working on a web app, so maybe I won't have to write this app!
